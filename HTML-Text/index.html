The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her window for about an hour ago. On her desk, lying between the copies of Nature, New Scientists, and all other scientists journals her work had appeared in, was a well thumbed copy of On The Road.

Oscar wilde quote proclaming that Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do. Although many considered her pioneering work, unravelling the secrets of the llama DNA to be an outstanding achievement. Mollydid think she had something better to do.
