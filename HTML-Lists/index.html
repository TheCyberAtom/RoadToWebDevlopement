Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in frying pan
  2. Mix the eggs and cream in the bowl
  3. after melting add egg and creams
  4. then scramble the egg in pan
